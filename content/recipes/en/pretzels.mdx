## Steps

1. Mix the water, yeast and sugar. Let rest for 5 minutes.
2. Knead the water with the flour, salt and melted butter.
3. Let rest for 10 minutes.
4. Preheat the oven to 205°C.
5. Bring the water and baking soda to a boil.
6. Divide the dough into 2 equal portions, roll them into 50 cm long ropes.
7. Shape the pretzels.
8. Poach them for 20 to 30 seconds. Sprinkle with fleur de sel. At this stage, they can be refrigerated for 24h.
9. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes.
