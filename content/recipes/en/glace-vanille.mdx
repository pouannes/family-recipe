## Steps
1. Split the vanilla pods and scrape out the seeds. Put the seeds and pods in a saucepan with the milk and cream over medium heat. Bring to a boil, turn off the heat, cover and infuse for 2 h.
2. Prepare an ice bath and set a bowl in it.
3. Reheat the liquids over medium heat.
4. Meanwhile, mix the sugar with the stabilizer, then whisk briskly into the yolks.
5. When the infusion is simmering, pour it gradually onto the yolks while whisking. Return everything to the saucepan and cook to 83°C.
6. Turn off the heat and stir continuously for 5 minutes.
7. Pour into the bowl over the ice bath and let cool for half an hour, stirring regularly.
8. Cover and refrigerate for 12–24 h.
9. Remove the vanilla pods, then churn.
