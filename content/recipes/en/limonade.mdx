## Steps
1. Zest the lemons with a microplane.
2. Add the sugar to the zest and stir until all the zest is coated. Leave it to rest while you do the next steps.
3. Juice the lemons.
4. Strain the juice into a jug, keeping only the liquid and discarding the pulp and seeds.
5. Drop the ice into the jug.
6. Pour the water onto the sugar-zest mixture and stir continuously until fully dissolved, about 1 minute.
7. Strain the syrup into the jug, discarding the zest.
8. Wait until the ice has dissolved, stirring occasionally, then serve.
<Tip>Use caster (superfine) sugar — it dissolves far faster than granulated in room-temperature water.</Tip>
