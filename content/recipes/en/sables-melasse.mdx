## Preparation

1. Cream the butter with the salt, sugar, and molasses.
2. Beat the egg and add it in 2 additions.
3. Add the flours, baking soda, and spices. Mix without overworking, until a dough forms.
4. Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 hour.
5. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C.
6. Roll out the dough to 3mm thickness and cut out shapes.
7. Bake for 12-15 minutes.

<Tip>Christmas spices include cinnamon, ginger, cloves, etc.</Tip>
