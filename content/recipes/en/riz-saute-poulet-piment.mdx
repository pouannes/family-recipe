## Steps

1. Cook the rice ahead of time, ideally 24h in advance, otherwise 1h.
2. Stir-fry the garlic, onion, shallot and bell pepper (all sliced) in oil over high heat.
3. When everything starts to brown, add the chicken cut into cubes.
4. After 3-4 minutes, add the fish sauce, sugar and sambal.
5. After 2 minutes, add the rice, separating the grains well.
6. When everything is nicely caramelized, serve topped with cilantro, peanuts and fried onion.
