## Raspberry Sorbet

1. Heat the water to 0°C, add the sugar.
2. At 40°C, add the stabilizer.
3. At 85°C, refrigerate for 19 hours.
4. Churn in an ice cream maker.

## Variation: Orange Sorbet

1. Heat the water to 0°C, add the sugar.
2. At 40°C, add the stabilizer.
3. At 85°C, refrigerate for 19 hours.
4. Add the freshly squeezed orange juice with pulp.
5. Churn in an ice cream maker.
