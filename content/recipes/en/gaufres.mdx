## Steps
1. Heat the waffle iron to maximum temperature.
2. Make a brown butter. Once it reaches the right color, let it cool with the milk and vanilla.
3. Meanwhile, whip the egg whites to stiff peaks, tightening them with the sugar.
4. When the liquids have dropped to 40-50°C, pour them slowly over the dry ingredients while whisking to avoid lumps.
5. Fold in the egg whites in 3 additions.
6. Cook, without greasing the plates.
