## Preparation

1. Make a brown butter.
2. Bring the milk and vanilla to a simmer.
3. Whip the egg whites to stiff peaks, gradually adding the sugar.
4. Preheat the waffle iron.
5. Mix the flour, salt and crumbled yeast.
6. Combine the butter and milk. Beat with the flour mixture until smooth.
7. Gently fold in the whipped egg whites.

## Cooking

1. Spray the waffle iron with cooking spray, cook 2 minutes on each side.
