## Steps
1. Cook the pasta per the package time, rinse under cold water and drain. Put it in a large bowl.
2. Cook the chicken breasts, let cool, then cut into roughly 2 cm cubes.
3. Halve the cherry tomatoes, finely slice the red onions and shred the basil.
4. Drain the artichoke hearts and quarter them. Drain the corn.
5. Add to the pasta: tomatoes, onions, basil, salad leaves, artichokes, corn and chicken.
6. Make the dressing: dissolve the salt in the vinegar, then add the mustard, lemon juice, honey, olive oil, garlic grated on a microplane and plenty of freshly ground black pepper. Blend with an immersion blender until creamy and emulsified.
7. Pour the dressing over the salad (adjust to taste — you may have some left over) and toss.
8. Crumble the feta over, scatter the parmesan shavings and serve well chilled.
<Tip>Raw garlic is potent — start with a tiny amount and add more only if needed.</Tip>
