## Steps

1. Mix the egg, sugar, and salt without whitening.
2. Add the flour all at once and mix until smooth.
3. Add the melted butter and stir.
4. Combine the milk, water, and vanilla.
5. Gradually pour the milk mixture into the rest of the batter, mixing until smooth.

<Tip>The vanilla extract can be replaced with orange blossom water or rum to taste. For La Haye griddles, set to 6-7.</Tip>
