## Steps

1. In a small saucepan, bring the oil, glucose, honey, and vanilla to a boil.
2. Mix the dry ingredients (rolled oats, seeds, dried fruits).
3. Add the liquid and stir until combined.
4. Pour into a 20x20cm frame.
