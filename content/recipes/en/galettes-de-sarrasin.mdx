## Steps

1. Mix the flour and salt. Make a well in the center.

2. Gradually pour in the water while mixing.

3. Once you have a smooth batter, add the egg.

<Tip>Let the batter rest for at least 1 hour in the refrigerator before cooking the galettes for a better result.</Tip>
