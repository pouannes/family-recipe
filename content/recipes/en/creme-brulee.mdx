## Steps

1. Preheat the oven to 100Â°C.
2. Combine the vanilla, cream, and milk in a saucepan and bring to a simmer.
3. Mix the yolks and sugar without incorporating air.
4. Gradually pour the cream mixture over the yolks, mixing well after each addition.
5. Divide among 3 creme brulee ramekins and carefully bake for 40 minutes.
6. Refrigerate for 4 hours, then sprinkle with brown sugar and caramelize with a torch.

<Tip>Orange blossom variation: replace the cream and milk with 175 g of heavy cream and 40 g of orange blossom water (without vanilla extract).</Tip>
