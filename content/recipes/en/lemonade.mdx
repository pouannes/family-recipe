## Steps
1. Zest the lemons and put them in a small saucepan with the water and sugar.
2. Bring to a boil, then strain and keep the liquid. Add the ice cubes immediately.
3. Juice the lemons, then strain.
4. Stir the juice into the syrup and serve once the ice has melted.
<Tip>Zest the lemons before juicing them — it's far easier on whole fruit.</Tip>
